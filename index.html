Technological Teaching
Site Author

Abstract
 The site goes in fine detail about how technology is taught in military schools. As technology expands by the day, every military school teaching these particular aspects have to stay on top of the latest technology. This article details how teachers go about lending information to the recruits and showing them the new advances in technology. It is crucial and vital that the recruits are made of aware of the technological advances of the times as it could very well determine how they are affected on the very battlefield they're on.
